How We Began

Through the process of moving, we noticed many sellers include furniture with the home sale, leaving new homeowners with the challenge of deciding what to keep and what to get rid of. On the other hand, many people are looking for quality furniture at affordable prices. This is where we come in! Our goal is to bridge the gap between these two scenarios, creating a seamless way for clients to get some extra cash for unwanted furniture while providing quality furniture and accessories at affordable prices to those who are looking.
